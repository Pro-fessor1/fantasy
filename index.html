FANTASY CREATIONS ASSOCIATION

A word from the CEO

CEO: Dan T

This is an educational association aiming at enabling its members to nurture their dreams as creators through developing their ideas, talents and skills backed by a supportive environment of like-minded people and access to knowledge and practice, thereby gaining exposure to the world of their dreams.

Our Core Values

Slogan

Dreams Brought Closer

Mission

United in nurturing dreams and ideas into reality

Vission

To become a remarkable dream nurturing association

MEMBERSHIP INTO THE ASSOCIATION

  1. Have a valid purpose for joining the association.
  2. Buy a membership t shirt and pay membership fee.
  3. Pay monthly contribution to the association treasury
  4. Obey the association rules and regulations

Association members reserve the right to join more groups/work independently as long as they fulfill the obligations of the FCA since the purpose of the association is mainly to provide an environment for growing their skills and talents.

THE DETAILS OF THE ASSOCIATION PURPOSE

To enable people find their matches (people with the same interests) hence teamwork, collective contribution of knowledge and ideas, capital plus provision of room for practice. This makes the association a perfect place for idea development and growth of talents and skills, leading to the acceleration of dream achievement.

We highly encourage specialization to enable concentration which leads to better performance and increasing chances of achievement. Therefore, each member is advised to choose and focus on one or two talents/skills of their interest.

Our association is dedicated to technology and entertainment. We therefore aim at developing skills and talents in the above mentioned fields.

The FCA intends to support its members in the development of the following talents and skills, with the help of professional trainings and courses. (The list is subject to updates)

BENEFITS OF BEING A MEMBER OF THE FCA

  • You’ll have got into a supportive environment of positive people where you won’t find negative vibes
  • You’ll have access to collective knowledge, ideas, group discussions, practical teamwork, professional courses, etc
  • You will have access to collective financial support
  • You will earn money. This will be through sale of creations made by the association. Members shall take a share of the association’s specific income.
  • You will get access to job/contract opportunities. Being our member makes you exposed hence easily noticed.
  • You will have taken a big step towards the achievement of your dream.
ASSOCIATION’S MAIN ACTIVITIES
  1. Attending expert trainings

  3. Group discussions
  4. Seminars
  5. Practical sessions
  6. Creation meetings
  7. Watching tutorials

INCOME STRATEGY
